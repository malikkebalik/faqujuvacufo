See What $24 Million Can Buy You In Oregon

September 02, 2024
blog

Mountain views? Check. Hundred of acres? Check. Countless residential amenities? Check.

Located in Sisters, Oregon, the R&B Ranch is the most expensive home on the market in Oregon. For $24 million, the new homeowners will revel in more than 500 acres of land and outstanding views of the Cascade Mountains.

The single-level main house was built in 2011 and has 14,666 square feet of interior space. This includes eight bedrooms, nine bathrooms, grand entry, stone fireplace, wet bar, formal dining, gourmet kitchen, butlers kitchen, wine room, theater, as well as a guest wing with four guest casitas.

On the grounds of the legacy ranch is an equestrian barn with saloon, indoor arena, racetrack, livestock barn, a 6,262-square-foot guest home, 3,727-square-foot managers home, stocked pond, and additional building sites. The property also includes more than 200 acres of water rights.
