The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers tease that RJ (Anthony Turpel) will soon have a life-changing encounter. He’s always admiring the models around the Forrester office, but he knows they’re out of his league. They still think he’s a cute kid, not a hot guy. Since there haven’t been any teens around, RJ has just tried to focus on reuniting his parents.

Luckily, RJ is about to have a new goal. The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers reveal that RJ will meet Coco Spectra (Courtney Grosbeck) and it will apparently be love at first sight. Once RJ and Coco start talking, they’ll immediately hit it off. That’s fantastic news for Sally (Courtney Hope) and the rest of the team at Spectra Fashions.

Coco is probably used to her sister’s sneaky schemes by now. There’s a good chance Sally has always been larger than life like her great aunt. B&B spoilers hint that Shirley (Patrika Darbo) and Sally will work together to come up with a plot. They’ll inform Coco that she’ll be carrying it out for them.

Coco may not like the idea of hurting her new crush, but her family might pressure her into it. Regardless, Coco will certainly be in the prime position to make something big happen for Spectra. The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers state that RJ will help Coco become an intern at Forrester Creations. He’ll be happy to score her a spot in the program.

Of course, RJ will feel like he can impress Coco with his influence at the company. He’ll also believe this will put Coco in his orbit more often. RJ will want the opportunity to get to know Coco a little better. Unfortunately, he won’t realize he’s being played.

What will Coco do once she’s in at FC? Will Sally and Shirley convince her to steal Forrester’s designs? If so, things could get tricky. As Coco falls for RJ, she may be torn between her loyalty to Spectra and her loyalty to the new guy in her life. If they officially begin to date, they’ll face major complications.

RJ would be hurt and angry if he knew Coco had a hidden agenda. However, there may come a time when Coco decides she can’t go through with it. She could prove her love by ditching the plan and refusing to help Spectra Fashions with their dirty tactics.

Would that be enough to keep RJ and Coco’s romance alive? B&B fans can expect some fun moments in this storyline. We’ll give you updates as other details emerge.
