What is Steve Smith's Net Worth?

Steve Smith is a Canadian comedian, actor, and writer who has a net worth of $5 million. Steve Smith was born in Toronto, Canada in December 1945. He studied engineering in college and then started writing, producing, and starring in the sketch comedy series Smith & Smith with his wife Morag Smith in 1979. He created the sitcom Me & Max in 1985 and then the TV series The Comedy Mill. Smith wrote for the sports comedy television series Offside on CTV and was a head writer for Global's series Laughing Matters.

His best known acting role came in the TV series The Red Green Show from 1991 to 2006 for which he also served as a writer. Smith also starred in the movies Delta Pi, The Chase, Sherlock: Undercover Dog, and Duct Tape Forever. He hosts the Steve Smith Playhouse and started a one man show called How to do Everything (From the Man who Should Know) in 2013. Smith has won two Gemini Awards and has been nominated for 21 others.
