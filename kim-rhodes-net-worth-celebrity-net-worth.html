What is Kim Rhodes' Net Worth?

Kim Rhodes is an American actress who has a net worth of $6 million. Kim Rhodes was born in Portland, Oregon in June 1969. From 2005 to 2008 she starred as Carey Martin on the television series The Suite Life of Zack and Cody and reprised the role from 2008 to 2011 in the TV series The Suite Life on Deck. Rhodes starred as Cindy Harrison on the soap operas Another World from 1992 to 1996 and As the World Turns from 2000 to 2001.

She has had a recurring role as Sheriff Jody Mills on the television series Supernatural since 2010 and as Vicki Bowen on the TV series Gortimer Gibbon's Life on Normal Street since 2014. Rhodes also starred as Rachel in the series Colony in 2016. She has starred in the films Christmas with the Kranks and Atlas Shrugged: Part 2. In 1998 she was nominated for a Soap Opera Digest Award for Favorite New Couple for Another World.
