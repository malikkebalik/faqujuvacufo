Todd Stashwick is an American actor. His acting career started in the 1990s. During his beginnings, he had guest appearances on numerous shows. In the 2000s, he had notable roles on the shows American Dreams and The Guardian. He has since appeared as a recurring cast on Still Standing and The War at Home. His first main role on television was on The Riches playing the role of Dale Malloy. He had also appeared in the films You, Me and Dupree, Live! The Air I Breathe and Surfer, Dude. In the 2010s, he played Eli on Heroes, Henry on Detroit 1-8-7, as well as appeared in 4 episodes of Men of a Certain Age. Most recently, he’s known for playing Henry Tate on Teen Wolf and Father Kieran O’Connell on The Originals. As of 2015, he stars as Deacon on 12 Monkeys.
