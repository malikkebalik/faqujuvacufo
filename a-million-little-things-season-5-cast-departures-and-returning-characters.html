A Million Little Things Season 5 Cast Departures and Returning Characters

A comprehensive guide to the 'A Million Little Things' Season 5 cast that might return or leave for the upcoming new season.

Published on May 24, 2022

2 min read

After that shocking A Million Little Things Season 4 finale, there are some obvious cast members who won’t return for season 5 on ABC. But, there’s also a whole ensemble of cast members who are sure to continue their storylines in A Million Little Things Season 5. Take a look at our breakdown of who might leave and who is sure to return for season 5, including the fate of Deliah Dixon (Stéphanie Szostak).

‘A Million Little Things’ cast returning for season 5

In A Million Little Things Season 5, David Giuntoli returns to the cast as Eddie Saville, but it doesn’t look like he’ll be with Anna Benoit when the show returns. Grace Park returns as Katherine Kim, and Tristan Byon continues to play Theo. Romany Malco portrays Rome Howard, and Christina Moses plays his wife, Regina. Allison Miller returns as Maggie Bloom and James Roday Rodriguez as Gary Mendez. Lizzy Green returns as Sophie Dixon. However, there are numerous recurring and guest characters who probably won’t return.

‘A Million Little Things’ cast leaving

Andrew Leeds won’t return to the A Million Little Things Season 5 cast as Peter Benoit because his character died. However, his ex-wife’s return is not as easy to determine. In the season 4 finale, Anna Benoit (Erin Karpluk) turned herself to the authorities for her involvement in Peter’s death. Eddie’s reaction to her arrest was not as dramatic as viewers expected. It looks like Karpluk might not return to the cast for season 5. 

In addition to Leeds’ departure, Ryan Hansen won’t return for A Million Little Things Season 5 either. He portrayed Maggie’s boyfriend, Camden Lamoureux, a Boston Bruins hockey player. However, Maggie and Cam broke up when she realized that she was still in love with Gary. 

Now that Gary and Maggie are expecting a baby together, it’s evident that his ex-girlfriend, Darcy (Floriana Lima), won’t return next season either. Darcy’s storyline wrapped up when she moved and reunited with her ex-husband.

Delilah returns for ‘A Million Little Things’ Season 5

A Million Little Things creator, DJ Nash, revealed that Stéphanie Szostak plans to return for season 5.

“We’re also excited to hopefully have Delilah return for a few episodes,” Nash told Deadline. “Stephanie has said she would love to come back, and we’ve missed her. So we’re excited to see the next chapter in that story.”

Delilah spent all of season 4 in France after she departed from Boston in the premiere. 

Cameron Esposito returns as Greta Strobe in the ‘A Million Little Things’ Season 5 cast

DJ Nash and executive producer Terrence Coli revealed that they would love to have Cameron Esposito as a series regular in the A Million Little Things Season 5 cast. Greta Strobe’s love story with Katherine became even stronger in the season 4 finale.

“We love Cameron,” Coli told TV Insider. “Adding her to the cast this season has not only brought a lot of comedy to the show but just been a great new voice for us to write for. The writers love her so much.”

Nash added that they hope to see Esposito a lot more next season, but they also want to be respectful of her comedy career.

A Million Little Things returns to ABC for season 5 in early 2023.
