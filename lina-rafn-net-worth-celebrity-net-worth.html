What is Lina Rafn's Net Worth?

Lina Rafn is a Danish singer, songwriter, and producer who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Lina Rafn was born in Copenhagen, Denmark in August 1976. At an early age she was a professional dancer and she made her professional singing debut in 1997. Rafn was a VJ on the Danish music video channel The Voice TV Danmark. She is part of the dance/pop group Infernal. Their most successful single has been "From Paris to Berlin" which reached #1 in Denmark and #2 in Finland, Ireland, and the UK.

Infernal released their debut studio album Infernal Affairs in 1998 and it was certified 2x Platinum in Denmark. They have released a total of six studio albums including From Paris to Berlin and Electric Cabaret which also went 2x Platinum in Denmark. Rafn has also recorded with Infernal member Paw Lagermann under the name Paw & Lina. She served as a judge for the first two seasons of the reality TV series Danish X Factor. Rafn is a part owner of the Copenhagen night club Klosteret.
