What is Liam Howlett's Net Worth?

Liam Howlett is an English record producer, musician, and songwriter who has a net worth of $10 million. Liam Howlett was born in Braintree, England in August 1971, He is best known for being the leader and co-founder of the electronic band The Prodigy. Howlett plays synthesizers, keyboards, drums, sampler, and turntables. The Prodigy formed in 1990 and released their debut studio album Experience in 1992 which reached #12 in the UK. They released the album Music for the Jilted Generation in 1994 and it reached #1 in the UK and Finland.

The Prodigy released the album The Fat of the Land in 1997 and it reached #1 in the US, the UK, Australia, Austria, Finland, Germany, Ireland, the Netherlands, and Norway. Their album Always Outnumbered, Never Outgunned was released in 2004 and reached #1 in the UK as did their albums Invaders Must Die in 2009, The Day Is My Enemy in 2015, and No Tourists in 2018. Their singles "Firestarter" and "Breathe" both reached #1 in the UK, Finland, and Norway. Liam Howlett married Natalie Appleton of All Saints in 2002.
