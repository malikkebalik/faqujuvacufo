Biography:

Jessica Green was born on February 11, 1993 in Tasmania, Australia. Her professional acting career started in 2012, starring as Kiki in the teen drama Lightning Point. She landed the role because of her martial arts skills. In 2014, she made her feature film debut in Rise. She next appeared in the independent movie Red Billabong.

Later in 2017, she made her Hollywood debut appearing with a small role in Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales. After this, she played Lexx in several episodes of Ash vs Evil Dead. The following year, she portrayed Cleopatra in the docudrama series Roman Empire. She’s now best known for her role as Talon in The CW’s The Outpost, for which she plays the lead.
