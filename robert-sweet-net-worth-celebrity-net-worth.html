What is Robert Sweet's Net Worth?

Robert Sweet is an American musician and producer who has a net worth of $1 million. Robert Sweet was born in Lynwood, California in March 1960. He is best known for being the drummer of the Christian heavy metal band Stryper. Robert and his brother Michael founded the band.

He is known for his drums being setup on the side of the stage instead of in the back. After the band Stryper broke up he played with King James and Shameless among other bands. Stryper formed in 1983 and broke up in 1993 before reuniting in 2003. They have released the albums The Yellow and Black Attack in 1984, Soldiers Under Command in 1985, To Hell with the Devil in 1986, In God We Trust in 1988, Against the Law in 1990, Reborn in 2005, Murder by Pride in 2009, The Covering in 2011, Second Coming in 2013, No More Hell to Pay in 2013, and Fallen in 2015.
