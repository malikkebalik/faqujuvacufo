What is Adam Lamberg's Net Worth?

Adam Lamberg is an American actor who has a net worth of $1.5 million. Born on September 14, 1984 in New York City, he is best known for his role as David "Gordo" Gordon on Lizzie McGuire, from 2001 to 2004. He became active in the entertainment industry in 1996 and concluded his career some twelve years later.
